Common questions

Everything you need to know before buying.

Does CleanList really not upload my data anywhere?
Correct. CleanList runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your CSV is parsed on your machine and never transmitted to any server. We don't have a backend that could receive it even if we wanted to.
How does the HubSpot preset work?
Select the HubSpot preset and CleanList automatically enables email deduplication, email lowercasing, phone standardization, and date formatting. It also removes hs_object_id, createdate, hs_lastmodifieddate, and other system columns — leaving only the contact data you need.
Is this a subscription? Will I be charged again?
No. $39 is a one-time payment. You will never be charged again. Access is tied to your browser via a session token — no account, no recurring billing, no expiry date.
Will my international phone numbers be corrupted?
No. CleanList only reformats US numbers — 10-digit and 11-digit starting with 1. International numbers (UK, EU, Brazil, Japan, everywhere else) are left exactly as-is.
Does it work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. CleanList works in any modern mobile browser. On iOS Safari the download flow is slightly different — you get a tap-and-hold link to save the file — but all cleaning features work identically. Android Chrome downloads normally to your Downloads folder.
Can I clean multiple files after purchasing?
Yes. After paying once, clean as many files as you want in the same browser. Click "Clean another file" after each download. Access persists in your browser's local storage until you clear it.
How do I restore access on a new device?
Your Lemon Squeezy purchase confirmation email contains your access link. Click it on any device to restore access. If you can't find the email, contact support@cleanlistapp.com with your order confirmation.
What's the largest file CleanList can handle?
Files up to 100MB. Most CRM exports are well under 10MB. For very large files (50,000+ rows) CleanList shows a progress bar and may take 10-20 seconds. Everything stays local — your browser is doing all the work.
What does "standardize dates" actually do?
CleanList detects 12+ common date formats and converts everything to YYYY-MM-DD. Salesforce and HubSpot timestamps like 2024-03-15T10:30:00Z are stripped to just the date. Unrecognized formats are left unchanged — your data is never silently corrupted.
How is this different from free CSV tools?
Most free tools require you to upload your data to their servers, don't understand CRM export formats, or are too complicated to use quickly. CleanList is the only tool with built-in presets for HubSpot, Salesforce, Apollo, and Mailchimp that processes everything locally — no upload, no account, no risk of client data leaving your machine.
